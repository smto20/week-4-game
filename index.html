Crystals Collector!

You will be shown a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals shown. When you click a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

If your total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game, you win!!

If your score goes above the random number, you lose.

The game restarts whenever you win or lose. Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
